Juan Camilo Cristancho Amaya

Colombian Researcher

PhD Student, School of Education, UC Irvine

My research interests revolve around the effects of vulnerable environments on

children in early childhood and school years, with emphasis in neighborhood effects

and the role of community level violence on cognitive and social-emotional outcomes.
